About IPS Sprinters


We are IPS SPRINTERS
IPS SPRINTERS, representing IPS Academy, Indore, is a team driven by a relentless passion for innovation and excellence. Our journey is defined by participation in the prestigious SAEINDIA Baja competition, where we shine in the A-baja, M-Baja, and E-Baja categories.

Who We Are
At IPS SPRINTERS, we are more than just a team; we are a powerhouse of innovation in the world of automotive engineering. Comprising forward-thinking students, we work tirelessly to design, build, and race off-road vehicles that not only meet industry standards but redefine them.

Our Mission
We believe in pushing boundaries, exploring new horizons, and fostering a culture of innovation. Our dedication to teamwork, problem-solving, and hands-on experience sets us apart.

Join the Journey
Embark on this exciting journey with IPS SPRINTERS as we strive to make a significant impact in the field of automotive engineering. With us, the future is fast and boundless.
